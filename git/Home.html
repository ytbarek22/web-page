Industry Extension

In a college context, the term "industry extension" may refer to the collaborative efforts between academic institutions and industries. This collaboration aims to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge gained in classrooms and the practical skills and requirements of various industries. It involves incorporating real-world industry practices into the academic curriculum,

Essentially, "industry extension in college" is about aligning academic programs with the needs and advancements of specific industries, ensuring that students are well-prepared and equipped with the practical skills and knowledge required to excel in their chosen fields after graduation. This approach helps students make a smoother transition from the academic environment to the professional world.
